Virtual Emergency Response Training

Github Repository

The Virtual Emergency Response Training (VERT) is dedicated to elevating preparedness and crisis management by utilizing virtual reality (VR) training and interactive learning to train rescue workers. Leveraging the immersive capabilities of VR, specifically with Meta Quest Family and hand tracking technology, VERT is tailored to address critical emergency scenarios

Scenerios

  • 1. Fire Suppression Training
  • 2. Evacuation and Rescue Training
  • 3. Emergency Vehicle Driving Training
  • 4. First Aid & Life Support Training
